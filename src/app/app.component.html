What's Amenities Map?

Amenities Map has been developed to solve a problem in my home country, Lebanon, but it is also at the same time a viable solution anywhere around the world.


Ever since COVID-19 hit, opening and closing hours of most amenities including car repair shops, clinics, restaurants, public parks...etc have been very inconsistent. Add to that, each one of those may have different rules and regulations that you are forced to abide by before entry.


Amenities Map makes your life easier by have direct communication with different franchises, shops, clinics, venues, and potentially municipalities, to provide accurate opening and closing times, required precaution wearables, and other valuable information. Everything right here at your fingertips, before you leave your home.

Features


App searches for reachable amenities around you

Get precise up-to-date opening and closing hours

Know about what each place's safety rules beforehand
